Snow Leopard

The snow leopard, or Panthera uncia, inhabits cold mountainous regions throughout Central and South Asia. It's a member of the Pantera genus along with the lion, tiger, leopard, and jaguar, and it's the only member of this genus that's incapable of roaring. Snow leopards have physical feautures that help them to thrive in cold environments, such as broad paws to more easily walk on snow and small ears to minimize loss of heat. As carnivores, snow leopards often hunt relatively large prey such as sheeps and goats.
